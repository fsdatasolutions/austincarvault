The Vault Experience

Beyond Storage.
A Sanctuary for Exceptional Vehicles.

We created Vault for collectors who understand that an extraordinary automobile deserves extraordinary care. This is not a parking facility. This is home.

Climate-Controlled Environment

Precision temperature and humidity control maintains optimal conditions year-round, protecting paint, leather, and mechanical components from environmental stress.

Daily Vehicle Attention

Every vehicle receives daily inspection and scheduled start-ups. Battery health, tire pressure, and fluid levels are monitored continuously by our dedicated team.

Personal Concierge

From coordinating service appointments to preparing your vehicle for weekend drives, your dedicated concierge ensures every detail is handled with precision.

Photo Documentation

Receive scheduled photo updates of your vehicle, capturing its pristine condition and providing peace of mind wherever you are in the world.

Strict Access Control

Biometric entry, 24/7 surveillance, and a strictly limited member roster ensure your vehicle remains secure and your privacy remains absolute.

Collector Community

Join a private community of like-minded enthusiasts. Exclusive events, drives, and gatherings connect you with fellow collectors who share your passion.
